## FAQ

**Do I need a credit card for Free?**
No.

**Can I upgrade anytime?**
Yes. Instant upgrade, prorated billing.

**What if I downgrade?**
Your extra projects are paused (not deleted) for 30 days. Takes effect at end of billing period.

**Do all tiers get all 42 detectors?**
Yes. Detection capability is the same across all tiers.

**What frameworks are supported?**
LangGraph, CrewAI, AutoGen, n8n, Dify, OpenClaw, Claude Code, and any OTEL-compatible framework.
